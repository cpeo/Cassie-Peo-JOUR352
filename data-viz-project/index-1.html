Top Spotify Songs and Their Weekly Changes

Findings from data from Kaggle tracking the top Spotify songs in 73 countries.

By Cassie Peo

This dataset presents the the Daily top songs on Spotify currently trending in 73 countries. About 37% of the songs in the data set are explicit and the changes in the weekly rankings of the songs show that a majority of songs either do not change in ranking or change very little each week.
